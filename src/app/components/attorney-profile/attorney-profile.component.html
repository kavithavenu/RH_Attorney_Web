Donald “Corky” Eby is a partner at Robinson & Henry, as well as the lead attorney for the firm’s civil litigation and real estate practices.
As a previous real estate company owner and operator, Don has firsthand experience dealing with landlord/tenant issues. Stemming from this background, Don is an experienced adviser and powerful advocate for clients facing any real estate challenge. He helps clients with the purchase or sale of commercial/residential property; real estate financing issues; land development or zoning problems; lease drafting or enforcement; landlord/tenant disputes or eviction; foreclosures; and HOA disputes.
Don is also a talented legal resource in the area of business and commercial law. He can provide guidance in issues involving acquisitions and mergers, joint ventures, entity formation, collections, franchises, and contract issues. For many of his small business clients, he also serves as general corporate council.
Don provides superior representation for all of his clients, but our nation’s veterans hold a special place in his heart. Don served in the U.S. Navy where he was assigned to aviation squadrons, aircraft carriers, and other special assignments. Today, he is proud to represent veterans on issues such as VA disability ratings, combat-related special compensation, discharge review, and other Veterans Administration claims.
